What is PictoData?
PictoData is a JavaScript library which takes data as input and creates a visual representation. The library includes the basics by allowing you to express data in the form of a classic bar graph. From there, PictoData really shines by letting you be creative with how you want to represent your data.

Check out our cool examples and see what PictoData is all about!
Why use PictoData?
The unique part of PictoData is that it allows you to have fun with how you want to represent your data. Web developers that want to showcase their data would not want to just put a bunch of numbers on their website. This is because the information would not look interesting and it may be difficult for viewers to find a correlation. Instead, a developer could use PictoData to turn their list of numbers into a stunning visual representation.
