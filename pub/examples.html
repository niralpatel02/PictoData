Bar graph

A bar graph creates bars proportionate to the numbers they represent.

const a = new Graph()
a.bar({
'Element': $('#bar'),
'Data': [42724, 38724, 40000, 36000],
'Labels': ['2013', '2014', '2015', '2016'],
'colours': ['#3e7bde', '#cf8934', '#954bc9', 'Aqua'],
'height': 400,
'width': 700
})
